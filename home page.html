SKCT students hostel is an integral part of the college campus. Hostel comparises of seven blocks with a capacity of 1200+ stduents. Hostel rooms are well maintained and hygienic, well spaced.

RO WATER FACILITY

Purified water from Reverse Osmosis Plant is utilized for cooking and drinking. Hot water is also provided for cooking and drinking purposes.

24X7 WI-FI FACILITIES

24 hours wi-fi facilities available Hostel
